Lesson 4: FOUR COMPUTER COMPONENTS OF A COMPUTER SYSTEM

The four computer components in a computer system are
software,hardware,data and peopleware.Software are
about the apps or programs that runs in a computer,
hardware refer's to the physical components like mouse
and keyboard,data refer's to the raw information
inputed or stored in the computer and people refers to
the users or people who are operating the computer
